An Automated ML Approach for Crop Recommendation and Yield Prediction.

Features

  1. Grow the right crops.
  2. Get the best yield.
  3. Procure better profits.
  4. Implement the right way to farm.

The Crop Connect project portrays two main features.

Crop Recommendation (CRM)


What is CRM ?


Crop Recommendation is suggestions about crops that are given to agriculturalists based on the pH values, types of soil, the nutrient contents present in the soil and other characteristics. The crops planted based on the suggestions yield maximum profit.

Crop Yield Prediction (CYP)


What is CYP ?


Crop yield prediction is an important aspect of agriculture that helps farmers make informed decisions about their crops. It involves estimating the number of crops that will be produced in a given area based on various factors such as soil type, weather conditions, and crop management practices.

How to Use?

Instructions:

Crop Recommendation


1. Test your soil for the nutrients and find out the N, P and K values of it and enter the values (mg/kg).

2. Enter the other important parameters relevant to ur farm accordingly as well.

3. Submit the form to be redirected to the result page where the prediction of the ideal crop to be grown in your farm is recommended.

Crop Yield Prediction


1. Test your soil for the nutrients and find out the N, P and K values of it and enter the values (mg/kg).

2. Enter the other important parameters relevant to your farm accordingly as well.

3. Submit the form to be redirected to the result page where yield of the crop chosen to grow in your farm is predicted.

View Crop Information


1. Find the crop you want to view detailed information on and click on it.

2. It will redirect to a detailed information page on the crop.

3. Also, check out the FAQ page to view the frequently asked questions.
